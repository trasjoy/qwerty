About Me

Hey there! My name is Princess Joy, and if I had to describe myself in a few words, I’d say I’m a dreamer with a serious case of wanderlust. My biggest goal for the future is to head abroad and start a career in a completely different part of the world. I’m really eager to step out of my comfort zone, experience new cultures, and see what the international job market has to offer.

When I’m not daydreaming about my future travels, you can usually find me falling down a rabbit hole of curiosity about the universe. I’m honestly fascinated by everything from the complexity of space to the mysteries of how the cosmos actually works—it’s just mind-blowing to think about how big everything is.

On the complete opposite end of the spectrum, I’m also a massive foodie. I absolutely love trying new snacks and meals, and even though I seem to have a bottomless stomach, I’ve somehow managed to stay skinny no matter how much I eat—which I definitely consider a superpower! I’m currently just enjoying my time here, learning new things every day, and working toward the day I finally get to pack my bags and head off on my next big adventure.
